About the Project

Create a Coaster is a collaborative project focused on turning a simple object into something meaningful. By combining 3D printing, design, and creativity, we aim to build products that are both functional and visually clean.

Design

Concepts are created and refined digitally before production.

Build

Each coaster is 3D printed and tested for durability.

Finish

Vinyl designs are applied for a custom final look.
